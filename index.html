    ESPNcricinfo

    ESPNcricinfo (formerly known as Cricinfo or CricInfo) is a sports news website exclusively for the game of cricket. The site features news, articles, live coverage of cricket matches (including liveblogs and scorecards), and StatsGuru, a database of historical matches and players from the 18th century to the present.

    Services

    Most cord cutters are familiar with Hulu's On-Demand service. However, they also offer a live TV service that includes ESPN. Hulu Live TV provides ESPN, ESPN 2, ESPNU, ESPNews, ACC Network, and SEC Network for $69.99/month.

    Owned By INDIA

    Cricinfo was founded in 1993 as an online community for cricket fans. It received major investment in 1999 from Indian IT firm Sify in return for a majority stake in the company, and in 2003 was bought by Mark Getty's cricket media business, the Wisden Group.
